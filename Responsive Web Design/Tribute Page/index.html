Ken Block, a visionary in motorsport and action sports

Renowned for his exceptional driving skills and innovative contributions to car culture.

Ken Block poses with his custom 1965 Ford Mustang Hoonicorn RTR V2, a 1,400 horsepower beast featured in his Gymkhana series.

Born on November 21, 1967, in Long Beach, California, Ken Block co-founded DC Shoes in 1994, significantly impacting the skateboarding and snowboarding apparel industry.

After selling DC Shoes, he pursued rally driving, quickly gaining recognition for his skill and daring stunts.

Block's Gymkhana video series showcased his precision driving in custom-built cars, notably the Hoonicorn Mustang, captivating millions worldwide.

Beyond motorsports, he was involved in various action sports, including skateboarding, snowboarding, and motocross.

Tragically, Block passed away in a snowmobile accident on January 2, 2023, leaving a lasting legacy in the automotive and action sports communities.
